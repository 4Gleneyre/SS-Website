Fundamental Skills/Characteristics of Bill Gates:

- Working ethic and decision making: Gates grew up in a family where education and hard work are valued. Along with that, he was taught that there was always a reward for winning and a consequence for losing. This shapes Gate’s ability to think critically, make decisions based on a holistic view of the current situation, and then execute.

- Focused and patient: Gates stayed on course to lead Microsoft to grow. He never let a lawsuit distract his focus.

- Determined and goal-driven: Over the years, Gates led Microsoft to keep expanding among the tech market by providing diverse services other than just Windows such as social media, office applications, gaming platform, cloud storage, mobile phones, voice assistant, etc. 

"To win big, you sometimes have to take big risks."
- Bill Gates

- Management Skills: Gates regularly met with program managers to hear new ideas, encourage anyone to defend their new proposals with good reasoning, and implement the idea. 

- Negotiating Skills: In 1981, Gates convinced IBM to allow him to have the licensing rights of MS-DOS. This brought Microsoft signficant fortune later. 

Summary: Gate’s unique leadership qualities, as listed above, along with his passion, vision, and knowledge well prepared his path to achieve success.
